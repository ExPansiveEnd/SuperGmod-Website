Super-Crates

The Super-Crate is an item / object, that can be obtained by typing "!crate" in the chat. When "e" is pressed while hovering over the box, it will open. What is inside will vary, from very useful (Rare), to absolute garbage, most of the time.










Mod Events

Mod Events, are unofficial server events that occur when moderators are online. These are wildly different each time, with a few "predictable" ones occasionally. These events usually take advantage of unique moderator permissions like teleportation and all of the sort. Past events included Console's boss brawl, "Retart Kart", "The Ghost Hunts", and others.
Player Hosted events also occur, several hosted by notable players like SoundBoard and Janky, with (insert town name here).
